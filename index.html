History and Vision and Mission

Pekalongan Batik Museum

The history of Pekalongan Batik Museum was established on 12 July 2006 and inaugurated by the 6th President of the Republic of Indonesia, Susilo Bambang Yudhoyono. The establishment of the Batik Museum is inseparable from the efforts of the people who are included in various communities of Batik lovers both in Pekalongan and on a national scale. Initially, the operation of the Batik Museum was under the Indonesian Chamber of Commerce and Industry (KADIN). In 2011, the management of the Batik Museum was transferred to the Pekalongan City Government where the operational funding is sourced from the Pekalongan City APBD. Under the management of the city government, the Batik Museum is in the form of UPTD Museum Batik.
